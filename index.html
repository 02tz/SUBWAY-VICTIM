From devotion to occupation

I am so fortunate to be born in an era of the Internet and rapidly developing technologies. My first introduction to a computer took place back in 1995. As a young boy, I was certainly attracted to computer animation and games. As a result, I found myself interested in the technical aspect of computers very early on. Being a student, I became an active user and blogger. Unfortunately, at that time I was studying jurisprudence (law) and was far from the world of web technologies. My first passion project was creating an independent website for a local hockey team that I played for.

After moving to Canada, I decided to make web development my career for life. I started my self-studying journey with books, online courses and by visiting several local workshops in Toronto. Currently, I am working on expanding my knowledge by acquiring various skills in Front-end Web Development, Responsive Web Design, JavaScript frameworks, CSS animation, and Precompilers.
