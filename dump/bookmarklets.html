Bookmarklet Collection

General Info

  • How to add Bookmarklets
    • Click the bookmark button (star), a box will pop up and click more. Where it says URL, copy and paste the bookmarklet code. Then click save and your bookmarklet should appear with other bookmarks. It is that simple.
